FAQ

Got
Questions?

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on complexity. A landing page: 1–3 days. Multi-page website: 1–2 weeks. Complex web applications: 3–6 weeks. I always provide a clear timeline before starting so there are zero surprises.

What do you need from me to start?

A brief project description, any design references you like, brand assets if you have them, and your content. Don't have everything yet? No problem — I can guide you through the process step by step.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes — all plans include a bug-fixing support window (10–30 days depending on plan). Beyond that, I offer ongoing maintenance packages. I'm also available on WhatsApp for quick questions anytime.

Will I own the source code?

Absolutely. On project completion and final payment, you receive full ownership of all source code, assets, and documentation — delivered cleanly via GitHub or your preferred method.

Can you work with my existing website?

Yes — bug fixes, new features, redesigns, performance improvements, platform migrations. Share your current site and I'll give you an honest assessment of what's needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

Bank transfer, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, PayPal and more. For larger projects I typically work 50% upfront, 50% on completion. We can always discuss what works best for your situation.
