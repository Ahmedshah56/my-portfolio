FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple landing page can be delivered in 1–3 days. A standard multi-page website takes 1–2 weeks. Complex web applications with custom functionality typically take 3–6 weeks. I always provide a clear timeline estimate before starting.

What do you need from me to get started?

To kick things off I'll need: a brief description of your project and goals, any design references or inspirations you like, your brand assets (logo, colors, fonts) if available, and the content you want on the site. Don't worry if you don't have everything — I can help guide you through it.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes! All projects include a bug-fixing support period after launch (10–30 days depending on the plan). Beyond that, I offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your site updated, secure, and running smoothly. I'm always available on WhatsApp for quick questions.

Will I own the source code after the project?

Absolutely. Once the project is complete and final payment is made, you receive full ownership of all source code, assets, and documentation. Everything is handed over cleanly via GitHub or your preferred file-sharing method.

Can you work with my existing website?

Yes! I regularly work on existing sites — whether it's fixing bugs, adding new features, redesigning sections, improving performance, or migrating to a new platform. Share your current site and I'll assess what's needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept payments via bank transfer, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, PayPal, and other methods. For larger projects, I typically work with 50% upfront and 50% on completion. We can discuss the payment structure that works best for you.
